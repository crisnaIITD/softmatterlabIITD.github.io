Soft Matter Lab

Introduction

Welcome to Soft Matter Lab, a research lab located in the Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Delhi. We focus on various aspects of soft matter and interfacial science.

Supervisor: Dr. Jayati Sarkar

Dr. Jayati Sarkar is an Associate Professor in the Chemical Engineering Department at IIT Delhi. Her research interests include:

  • Instabilities, Adhesion, Debonding, Dewetting, and Pattern Formation of Soft Thin Films
  • Interfacial Science
  • Nanoparticle-selfassembly
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Self Organization of Complex Fluids and Granular Materials

Lab Location and Facilities

Our lab is situated in Block I, Department of Chemical Engineering. The lab number is 336, located on the third floor.
